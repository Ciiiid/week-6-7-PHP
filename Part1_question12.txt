$matrix = [
	[1, 2, 3],
	[4, 5, 6],
	[7, 8, 9]
];
echo $matrix[1][2]; //Access a specific element in the multidimensional array

Question 12: What is the output of echo $matrix[1][2];?
Answer:
	6, since $matrix[1] refers to the second row, and $matrix[1][2] refers to the third element of the second row which is 6
